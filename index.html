Projects

Shadow of the Sanitorium

Shadow of the Sanitorium is a 2D roguelike prototype focussing on procedural generation. My primary focus on this project was player, enemy, and stat systems.

Player and enemy states were both implemented using a State pattern. Enemy stat and collectable item data were stored using ScriptableObjects, allowing the addition of new objects to be added quickly.

OverenLUMBERed

OverenLUMBERed is an incremental game. As the player gains more money from chopping down trees, they can upgrade their equipment and hire employees to help with their work. After a while, the spawn rate of the trees slows to a halt, eventually putting the game in an unwinnable state.

Winner of the Games Job Fair Spring 2023 - Unity Programming Challenge.

itch.io github

Your Kickstarter Survives

Your Kickstarter Survives is a work in progress based on the mechanics of the popular game Vampire Survivors and the podcast Your Kickstarter Sucks.

This project relies heavily on ScriptableObjects for weapon and enemy data, as well as utilizes object pooling in order to maintain performance with such a large number of enemies used at the same time.
